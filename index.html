Our Coverage

As of now, we only cover the Miami region due to its abundance of light pollution and our lack of resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the fight against light pollution by developing a highly accurate regression-based
machine learning model that accurately predicts light pollution levels and highlights the impact on human health,
wildlife, and ecosystems. We aim to inspire action and raise awareness, empowering communities to reduce light pollution for future generations.
